Directions

How to Get Here

From the Ocean City Boardwalk

5-minute walk or drive. Head south on Baltimore Ave to Talbot Street, turn right toward the water. We're at the dock right under the bridge.

From Rt. 50 Bridge (coming into OC)

Turn right on Talbot Street immediately after crossing the bridge. Look for The Angler Restaurant sign. We're on the north side of the building at the waterfront dock.

From Rt. 90 Bridge

Head south on Coastal Highway (Rt. 1). Turn right on Talbot Street and follow it to the waterfront. You'll see the bridge overhead and The Angler Restaurant on the right.

From Delaware / Fenwick Island

Head south on Coastal Highway into Ocean City, MD. Continue past all the hotels to Talbot Street (just before the Rt. 50 bridge). Turn left, follow to waterfront.

Location FAQ
