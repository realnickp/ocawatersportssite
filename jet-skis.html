The Difference

This Isn't Your Average Jet Ski Rental.

While other Ocean City companies keep you circling the bay in a confined, buoyed-off zone — we take you on an adventure. From the inlet, you ride 6 miles down to the Verrazano Bridge along Assateague Island National Seashore — one of the longest open-water jet ski routes on the East Coast.

Our signature 6 Mile Ride takes you straight past wild horses that have roamed since the 1600s, dolphins breaking the surface off the bow, and sandbars rising out of nowhere. You get 70 minutes on the water, not 60. Because we ride too, and we know what riders actually want.

Know Before You Go

Jet Ski FAQ

Our Sea-Doo GTX skis are capable of 40–42 mph on open water. Speed depends on conditions, weight, and currents. You have full throttle freedom — we're not running alongside you telling you to slow down.

Our jet ski route runs south from the inlet along Assateague Island down to the Verrazano Bridge — a 6-mile stretch of wide open water. You'll ride past wild horses, dolphins, hidden sandbars, and some of the most scenic coastline on the East Coast. This is a one-direction ride south, not a circle — the way jet skiing should be. We brief you on the full route before you launch.

You must be at least 16 years old to operate a ski. Riders under 21 require a parent or guardian present at the time of rental. All riders must sign a liability waiver.

You can absolutely ride with more than one person. Each ski holds 1–3 riders with a 450 lb maximum weight limit per ski. We have up to 10 skis available per time slot. Coming with a big group? Call us at 410-629-RIDE — we'll make sure we have what you need.
