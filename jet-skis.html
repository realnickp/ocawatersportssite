Know Before You Go

Jet Ski FAQ

Our Sea-Doo GTX skis are capable of 40–42 mph on open water. Speed depends on conditions, weight, and currents. You have full throttle freedom — we're not running alongside you telling you to slow down.

Our riding area covers the entire bay from Verrazano Bridge to the Rt. 90 Bridge — the largest riding area in Ocean City MD. You can ride to Assateague Island, cruise past Seacrets, Fish Tales, or Fager's Island. We'll brief you on all boundaries at check-in.

No. Your 70 minutes starts the moment you hit the water — not when you walk in the door, not when you sign waivers. That's why we ask you to arrive 45 minutes before your scheduled time. Show up early, get the full 70.

You must be at least 16 years old to operate a ski. Riders under 21 require a parent or guardian present at the time of rental. All riders must sign a liability waiver.

Each ski accommodates 1–3 people depending on total weight. We have up to 10 skis available per time slot. Coming with a big group? Call us ahead of time — we'll make sure we have what you need.

None required. We give every rider a full orientation at the dock before you launch — controls, safety, boundaries, the route. If you can drive a car, you can ride a jet ski. Most first-timers are flying within 10 minutes.

Swimwear, sunscreen (apply before arriving — it washes off fast on the water), and a sense of adventure. We provide life jackets. Leave valuables, car keys, and phones in your vehicle or bring a waterproof case.

All sales are final. No refunds given. In the rare event that we cancel due to extreme weather or mechanical issues on our end, you'll receive a full credit to rebook at your convenience.

Yes — there's a free white vinyl fence lot at the corner of Talbot & Caroline Street, just steps from our dock. If it's full, street parking on Talbot and Caroline is usually available. See our Contact page for the full parking guide.
