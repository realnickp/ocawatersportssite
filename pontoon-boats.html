Private Charter

Your Boat. Your Rules.

This isn't a tour. There's no captain telling you where to go or how long to stay. You rent the boat — the whole thing, just for your group — and you captain your own adventure across Ocean City Maryland's entire bay.

Want to beach directly on Assateague Island and let the wild horses walk up to you? Do it. Want to bar hop to Seacrets or Fish Tales from the water like a local? Pull up and dock. Want to drop anchor on a sandbar and make it a full-day party in the sun? That's the most popular move on the bay all summer.

The Assateague Island shoreline is a 37-mile protected National Seashore. Pull your pontoon right up to the bay-side sandbars — some just inches deep. No parking lot. No boardwalk. Just you, the wild horses, and one of the most untouched stretches of coastline on the East Coast.

Other boat rental companies won't let you go to Assateague. That's exactly why we're different.
