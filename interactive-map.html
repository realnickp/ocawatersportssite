What Is the Interactive Map?

We built our own scrollytelling bay map so you can preview the entire Ocean City watersports experience from your phone. It's a guided, visual walkthrough of the water — not a static pin map.

Pick a mode (jet ski or pontoon), scroll down, and the map flies you stop by stop along the route. At every landmark you get photos, drone footage, and context: what you'll see, where you can stop, and what makes each spot special.

  • Every stop on the 6 Mile Ride.The full southbound jet ski route to Assateague Island — landmarks, sandbars, and wild horse spotting zones.
  • Every pontoon destination.Restaurants, hotspots, hidden sandbars, and bay cruising routes you can reach on a boat rental.
  • Photos & videos at each pin.See the actual views before you launch. No guessing, no generic stock photos.
  • Works on your phone on the water.Keep it open while you ride — it's a preview and a guide.
